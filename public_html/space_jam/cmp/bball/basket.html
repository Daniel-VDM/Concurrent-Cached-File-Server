If you have the latest Quicktime plug-in for your browser, you will see the QTVR below.




Use your mouse (with the button down) to move around within the movie. Use your "control" key to zoom out, and your OPTION key (for Macs) or SHIFT key (for PCs) to zoom in.

If you do not have the appropriate plug-in to view the Quicktime VR file within your browser, download the QTVR file in Macintosh or Windows format and play it off-line with the Apple QTVR Player.
